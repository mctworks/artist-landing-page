Commission Terms & Rules!

Immediate payment is required upfront after discussion of desired descriptions. I send the first sketch within a few days after receipt of payment. I allow up to three minor revisions; anything major will be an additional charge or cancelled entirely as I see fit.

The turnaround time is between 1-3 days to about a few weeks depending on my work schedule. I draw on a part-time, often rare basis.

I allow up to 3 free revisions. If the client is not satisfied after the 3, the commission is cancelled and the customer will be refunded promptly.

I will draw: Fanart, original characters, anything cute and not overly complicated.

I will NOT DRAW: anything weird or NSFW, fetishes, mechas, animals/furry stuff, anything I feel will be overly complicated.

Refunds are as follows: I will refund if I fail to complete a commission at a reasonable time or complete deadlines, I feel that a client is harassing or too pushy with checking the status of a commission. I don't draw every single day of the week, I am not a personal drawing machine. Inspiration comes and goes, and life happens, but I will communicate as best as I can.

Commissions are physical pieces of art; I do not do any digital art. They are drawn on Bristol paper with a protective backing and plastic wrap. All pieces are shipped with Priority Shipping. A tracking number will be provided. The postal system has its issues but about 90% of the time you will get your piece. If for some reason it gets lost, I will do my best to accomodate!

I can deliver if a client is in the New Haven Area!
